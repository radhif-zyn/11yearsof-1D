We Are One Direction

One Direction, often shortened to 1D, are an English-Irish pop boy band formed in London, England in 2010. The group are composed of Niall Horan, Liam Payne, Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson, and Zayn Malik. The group signed with Syco Records after forming and finishing third in the seventh series of the British televised singing competition The X Factor in 2010

One Direction Timeline

As fans across the world mark 10 years of One Direction, we take a look back at a timeline of the band

July 2010 The X Factor, One Direction was formed by Simon Cowell when Louis Tomlinson, Niall Horan, Harry Styles, Liam Payne and Zayn Malik – who all auditioned as solo artists – were put together.

On December 2010 While their immense fame and success might have something to say about it, One Direction did not actually win their series of the X Factor. The band came in third place in the finals.

February 2012, One Direction released their debut single What Makes You Beautiful in 2012. The song became a hit and solidified their place as the UK's next top boy band.

August 2013, the band released a documentary film titled This Is Us. The film showed behind the scenes footage of the band's time on tour, as well as some personal moments from all five of them.

November 2013, the band release their third album, Midnight Memories. Their first album was titled Up All Night and the second Take Me Home. Their final album was titled Made In The A.M.

After five years in the band, Zayn announced he was leaving One Direction on March 2015. In a statement announcing the news, the band wrote: "After five incredible years Zayn Malik has decided to leave One Direction."

Just months after Zayn left, the rest of One Direction announced they were going on a hiatus on December 2015. All members have gone on to pursue solo careers.

Album Sales

A total of more than 6.49 million albums have been sold in the United States. These sales are a total of 4 albums sold in the United States.

Downloaded Song

More than 23.7 million songs have been downloaded by One Direction fans in the United States.

Top 10 Billboard Hot 100

There are 4 songs that are included in the top 10 of the Billboard Hot 100, these are Best Song Ever, Live While We're Young, What Makes You Beautiful, and Story of My Life.

Billboard Hot 100

There are about 19 songs that entered the Billboard Hot 100 including the single titled Night Changes.

Top Artist

In 2014, One Direction became the first band to be named Billboard's Top Artist since Destiny Child in 2001.

Youtube Viewers

A total about 3 Billion viewers have been achieved by One Direction through video clips uploaded on the Youtube site.

Tour Income

In 2014, One Direction earned $290 million in material coffers for their world tour.

Documentary Film Revenue

A total of 28 million US dollars were obtained by Harry Styles and his friends through their documentary, One Direction: This Is Us.

Billboard Music Awards

One Direction won awards at the 2013 Billboard Music Awards for 3 categories namely Top New Artist, Top Duo/Group, and Top Pop Artist. In addition they also won 5 awards at the BRIT Awards and 5 awards at the American Music Awards.
